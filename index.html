Alongside my classmates, I had the pleasure of contributing hard labor for renovation of orphanage including new classrooms/dormitories and organizing activities and games for orphans with physical and mental disabilities. Help Portrait

Help Portrait is a global movement to give back to the community by offering portraits of individuals and families. I was part of the outreach team that reached out to households in the community to invite them to the event, and I also helped families prepare for their photographs. Help Portrait has been an especially meaningful experience, as many of the families I interacted with could not even afford a family portrait.
